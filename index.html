Research

Impact of Post-Disaster Debris Management on Communities

Advisor: Dr. Jenna Tilt · Oregon State University

My dissertation investigates the environmental, social, and logistical impacts of post-disaster debris management on affected communities. Using Hurricane Helene and Milton (2024) as case studies, I employ a mixed-methods approach combining in-depth interviews, community surveys, geospatial analysis, and remote sensing to understand how debris management decisions shape recovery outcomes, community well-being, and environmental justice. This research aims to inform more equitable and effective disaster debris policies for vulnerable coastal and urban communities.
